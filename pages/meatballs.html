Meatball Recipe

Tastiest Homemade Meatball Recipe

Thereâ€™s nothing quite as satisfying as a pasta bowl cradling perfectly cooked, twirled and tangled, steaming spaghetti coated in a vibrant, garlicky, San Marzano marinara sauce, all piled high and layered with homemade meatballs throughout! Talk about comfort food at its proudest!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup whole milk,or more if needed
  • 1 lb 85% lean ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 tbsp choped fresh basil
  • 2 tbsp choped fresh parsley
  • 1 cup finally shredded Parmesan cheese
  • salt,pepper

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl stir together bread crumbs and milk while adding more milk 1 tbsp at a time to moisten bresd fully. Let rest while you prep ingredients (or at least 5 minutes)
  2. To breadcrumb mixture add beef, pork, eggs, onion, garlic, basil, parsley cheese and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Gently toss mixture and break meat up with fingertips until ingredients are evenly mixed.
  4. Shape mixture with greased hands into even size meatballs.
  5. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and spray non-stick pans with Pam or brush with oil.
  6. Bake meatballs for about 8-12 minutes.
  7. Stir into warmed marinara sauce

Enjoy!!!
